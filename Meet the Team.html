Our team

Robert
Web Designer

Robert is a fourth-year student majoring in Computer Science. He has a great passion for mathematics and programming. Robert’s job for the project was to put together the website. He has well over 10 years of experience in programming with procedural and object-oriented languages, but has never touched any sort of web design. He took this opportunity to learn more about the world of web design as well as learning all about digital humanities. 

Sarah
Project Manager

Sarah is a second-year student majoring in Design Media Arts and minoring in Digital Humanities. Her background is in marketing, entrepreneurship, and graphic design, but she is very interested in pursuing a career in UX Design and Product Management. As the project manager, she oversaw the overall progress of the project by creating and submitting documents as a team, setting deadlines, and ensuring harmonious and effective communication between team members. Other than managing the flow of the project, she also contributed to drafting the narrative, critiquing the data, and analyzing data visualizations. 

Ruyi
Data Specialist

Ruyi is a third-year student majoring in Data Theory and minoring in Digital Humanities. Her background is mainly in mathematics and data science; she is interested in obtaining a master degree in data science or machine learning and a career in related areas. As the data specialist of the team, she worked on extracting, cleaning, and combining all data used for the project, and she also provided a comprehensive analysis to the observations and contributed to the critiques of the dataset. In addition to the data analysis, she generated the visualization for “Correlation between Airbnb density and the socioeconomic status of each neighborhood” and “Variation of Airbnb price across different neighborhoods”, and worked on the following analysis of these research questions. She also developed the timeline that focuses on the historical and social background for the project and aided the narrative formation. 

Maggie
Editor

She is passionate about applying her technical skills to real-world information and data to create positive social impact, something she hopes to explore with digital humanities. In addition to working with the team on group assignments, as the editor for the project, she made sure that the project as a whole was cohesive in terms of design, organization, and writing style.
